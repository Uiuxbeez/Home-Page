About Company

Pioneering in every Sphere of Business Since 1967

Mr K K Ramaswamy dream was to build a business empire with the meagre resources he had saved during his decade long employment with PSG Industrial Institute, Coimbatore, India.

Features of Sharp Tools

Thanks to his vision, the next five decades saw the company grow from strength to strength. Today, along with its associate organisations, Sharp Tools employs more than 300 highly skilled employees, and offers world class machinery for manufacturing the entire gamut of welding consumables, high efficiency electric motors, state of the art electric water pumps, automatic water level controller-cum-motor pump protector, and speciality welding electrodes.
